About Us

Established in 2020, Brewed Awakening is more than just a coffee shop - it's a community hub where coffee lovers gather to enjoy expertly crafted beverages and delicious breakfast offerings. Our passion for quality coffee and exceptional service drives everything we do.

We source our beans from sustainable farms and roast them in-house to ensure the perfect cup every time. Our skilled baristas are dedicated to creating the perfect coffee experience for each customer who walks through our doors.
